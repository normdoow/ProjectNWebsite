What's this Blog About?

Future captains of Duel Lagoon, we are so excited for you to be checking out our blog! We’re Noah Bragg and Drew Bidlen, the guys behind the game. We’re full-time college students, which means that the development process will take longer than we’d like, but our confidence in the game’s potential will drive us! We think that Duel Lagoon can be the next big mobile game, and we want you to come along on the development journey with us!

We have two major goals for this blog. First, we want to give insight into the app development process to jump start your own creations! We plan to write on everything from the programming itself to the user interface, but in summary, how to turn an idea into a real app. Second, we’ll gradually unveil information about our game, making sure that all of our readers are up to date on the project! Expect to see videos, updates, and maybe even surveys that will allow you to impact development decisions! If you’re onboard, sign up for our Beta launch, and you’ll be the first to know when we have a build ready for you to play! Please comment below, we’d love to answer your questions and get your thoughts on the game!

Drew Bidlen:

Currently, I'm a junior electrical engineering student at Cedarville University. I really enjoy both electronics and software, and hope to work in consumer robotics after graduation. Outside of school and working on Think or Sink, I enjoy playing doubles beach volleyball tournaments, algorithmic stock trading, and spending time with friends and family.
